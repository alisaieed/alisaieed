## About me

I'm a full-stack engineer with **5+ years** building scalable web applications, multi-tenant SaaS platforms, and enterprise ERP systems — primarily across **Laravel**, **Vue.js**, **Python**, and **Odoo**.

My work sits at the intersection of **software engineering** and **business operations** — I don't just build features, I model the business flows they represent. That's let me deliver end-to-end across warehousing, logistics, e-commerce, manufacturing, and exhibition-management domains.

## Key projects

### 🏭 Stokvera — Multi-Tenant WMS SaaS *(SAAR Limited Co.)*
> A production warehouse management platform serving 15+ tenant accounts, built from the ground up on Laravel + Vue.js.

**What I built:**
- **Shipments module** — end-to-end picking, packing, and dispatch automation with drag-and-drop packaging UI, carrier API integrations, label generation, and webhook-based shipment tracking
- **Plans & Subscriptions module** — tenant plan tiers, usage limits, and billing enforcement
- **RBAC system** — role-based permissions letting tenants build custom roles and control user-level access
- **Performance layer** — MySQL schema optimization and query indexing, cutting execution time by ~35%

`Laravel` `Vue.js 3` `Vuetify 3` `Pinia` `MySQL` `REST APIs` `Webhooks` `RBAC` `Multi-Tenancy`

---

### 🛒 Shella Food — Multi-Vendor Logistics Platform *(Portal Logistics)*
> E-commerce and logistics platform serving 400+ vendors and ~12,000 orders/month.

**What I built:**
- **Drop-shipping engine** — automated provider-selection algorithm routing each order to the most profitable supplier in real time
- **Real-time tracking** — WebSocket-based order tracking and client–vendor–courier chat for 2,000+ daily active users
- **Saudi TGA integration** — order and vehicle tracking compliance with a dedicated monitoring dashboard
- **Dynamic pricing engine** — multi-level tax and pricing rules (vendor / category / item) for accurate POS calculation

`Laravel` `WebSockets` `REST APIs` `Redis` `MySQL` `CI/CD` `Load Balancing`

---

### 🏭 Odoo 15 Manufacturing ERP Migration *(Powerhouse Iraq)*

Configured MRP, BOMs, and work center routing across 6 customized modules in 6 months — cutting operational processing time by **45%**. Trained 50+ employees across production and finance, achieving **100% user adoption**.

`Odoo 15` `Python` `XML` `PostgreSQL` `MRP` `Manufacturing`
